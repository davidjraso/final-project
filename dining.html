Dining

St. Louis is such an eclectic food city so the options for broadening your horizens and trying something new are endless. Here are some suggestions of where to eat both on, near, and away from campus.

There are numerous different places on campus to eat. All First-Year Students live on the South 40 which is the home of the Bear's Den. It is a large dining facility with various cuisines, meals, and snacks to enjoy. All other dining locations are spread out on main campus and are centralized around a certain discipline of study, so based on your major you can find the best place to get your food.

The Delmar Loop is a convenient and delicious way for students to leave the bubble and have a great meal. There is an overpass connected to main campus which allows for quick access and there are a number of different and cost effective places to enjoy. A few of the highlights are listed above with their specific cuisines, but you can Visit the Loop online to see all the options. There's not just food, there are also different shops to check out, a movie theater, and a bowling alley that allow for endless entertainment.
