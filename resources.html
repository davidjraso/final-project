There are a number of clubs you can join at Wash U. Check out WU Churn if you have a hand for churning butter. Or if you have a fascination with beekeeping, Bee School is the club for you. There are a number of recreational sports clubs you can join like Rock Climbing and Kendo, or if you have a good palate give Campus Kitchen (image above) a try. These are just a few of the many clubs that exist, so explore your options and try something new!

Across campus there are a number of different organizations. There are many cultural groups, like Ashoka, the Southeast Asian student group, the Association of Black Students, and the Asian American Association to name a few. There are also various religious organizations like Hillel and Catholic Student Center (image above), and Pride Alliance is another great way to get involved in a great community. To take part in a national organization, join Dance Marathon to have some fun and raise money for a great cause. There are so many other fantastic organizations you can join at Wash U.

If you want to search for more clubs, click on Student Union to visit the SU Group Directory. Here you can search for key words that interest you and it will match you with your ideal clubs and organizations.

Or if you have a group in mind and want to contact its members or get more information, click on Portfolio to go to a special Wash U website that has all the information you could need on every club and organization at the university.

Association of Latin American Students

ALAS is the premier cultural group for students of Latin American descent at Wash U.

Bhangra Team

The Bhangra Team is one of the Indian dance groups on campus. They are directed by Ashoka, the South East Asian student group.

Campus Kitchen

Campus Kitchen is a volunteer student organization that aims to address food insecurity and waste.

Campus Y

Campus Y allows students to grow as leaders in programs run through the YMCA.

Catholic Student Center

The Catholic Student Center, run by Father Gary, is a non-denominational resource for all christian students.

Leaders in Interpersonal Violence Education

Live is an organization which aims to hold facilitations to promote awareness and prevention of sexual violence.

Mama's Pot Roast

Mama's Pot Roast is an improv/comedy group that holds various sketch shows throughout the year.

Social Programming Board

SPB is one of the largest student organization which puts on comedy shows, musical performances, and much more throughout the year.

Student Union

Student Union is the government on campus which allocates budgets to more than 200 student groups and puts on a variety of events.

Synapse

Synapse is a student organization focusing on neuroscience related activities both on campus and in the St. Louis community.

WU Churn

WU Churn is the premier buttern churning club on campus.
